Sweat Guru is a better way to discover fitness

My involvement from the get-go included working with the Cosmic Team and the Sweat Guru Co-Founders to develop a brand from the ground-up and create a scalable application and marketing items. Sweat Guru Co-Founders their iOS developer and a cosmic that consisted of 2 designers and 1 developer. I helped develop the sweat guru brand and carry that on through out their product design, we started by learning about what the company’s main goal and strategy was and created a visual language that reflected a soft and approachable image. After learning more about the company, we began conceptualizing different directions and executions of the logo.

The Final Result

We decided to tackle the logo at 2 different stages. We kept it simple by creating a friendly word-mark from scratch, simultaneously we worked with an illustrator to develop a mascot that’ll represent the sweat guru company as a living mark. While this living mark will be used through out marketing events and promoting fitness, the word mark will be face-fronting the business side and mostly be present in the app.
